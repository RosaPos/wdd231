Level Up English Institute Site Plan

Individual Website Project — WDD 231

Site Name

Level Up English Institute

This name was selected because it clearly represents an English education business focused on helping students improve their English level through online classes, structured lessons, and personalized support. The phrase “Level Up” communicates progress, growth, and confidence in learning.

Optional domain: levelupenglishinstitute.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an organized and welcoming online presence for Level Up English Institute. The site will explain the institute’s teaching approach, present English programs for children, teenagers, and adults, share the benefits of online English classes, and provide a contact or enrollment form for interested students and families.

The website will also support future dynamic features such as program cards generated from a data file, student benefits, class information, and interactive enrollment options.

Scenarios

  • What English classes are available for children, teenagers, or adults?
  • How can I contact Level Up English Institute to ask about schedules and enrollment?
  • What are the benefits of taking online English classes instead of in-person classes?
  • How does Level Up English Institute support beginner students who need personalized help?

Color Scheme

The color scheme uses professional education colors that communicate trust, growth, clarity, and energy.

Navy Blue

#13294B

Used for headings, footer, navigation, and strong brand elements.

Bright Blue

#2F80ED

Used for buttons, links, highlights, and calls to action.

Gold Accent

#F9C74F

Used for accents, small highlights, and visual emphasis.

Light Background

#F5F8FF

Used for page backgrounds and soft content sections.

Typography

Montserrat

Montserrat will be used for headings, navigation labels, buttons, and important section titles because it is clear, modern, and professional.

Lato

Lato will be used for body text, descriptions, program details, and form text because it is readable and friendly for online learning content.

Planned Website Pages

  • Home: introduction, main benefits, call to action, and featured programs.
  • Programs: dynamic program cards for children, teenagers, adults, and conversation classes.
  • Enroll: contact form, enrollment information, and student inquiry details.

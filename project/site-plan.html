WDD 231 Individual Website Project

Level Up English Institute Site Plan

A playful, modern, and clear website plan for an online English institute that helps students improve their English with confidence.

Site Name

Level Up English Institute

This name was selected because it clearly represents an English education business focused on helping students improve their English level through online classes, structured lessons, and personalized support. The phrase “Level Up” communicates progress, growth, confidence, and achievement.

Optional domain: levelupenglishinstitute.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an organized and welcoming online presence for Level Up English Institute. The site will explain the institute’s teaching approach, present English programs for children, teenagers, and adults, share the benefits of online English classes, and provide a contact or enrollment form for interested students and families.

The website will also support future dynamic features such as program cards generated from a data file, student benefits, class information, and interactive enrollment options.

Scenarios

  • What English classes are available for children, teenagers, or adults?
  • How can I contact Level Up English Institute to ask about schedules and enrollment?
  • What are the benefits of taking online English classes instead of in-person classes?
  • How does Level Up English Institute support beginner students who need personalized help?

The color scheme uses a playful purple theme inspired by the Level Up logo and a gaming-style landing page, while keeping the clean and simple structure of an educational site.

Typography

Fredoka

Fredoka will be used for headings, navigation labels, hero titles, and buttons. It feels friendly, modern, and suitable for a playful learning brand.

Nunito

Nunito will be used for body text, descriptions, program details, scenarios, and form text because it is rounded, readable, and comfortable for educational content.

Planned Website Pages

  • Home: welcome message, benefits, call to action, and featured programs.
  • Programs: dynamic program cards for children, teenagers, adults, and conversation classes.
  • Enroll: contact form, enrollment information, and student inquiry details.

Design Direction

The final website will combine a playful purple visual identity with a simple educational layout. The design will use large headings, rounded cards, friendly illustrations or icons, clear calls to action, and smooth movement effects that support learning without distracting the user.
